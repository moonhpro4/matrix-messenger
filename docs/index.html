FEATURES

Everything a messenger should do

No bloat, no number required, no tracking beyond what you choose to back up yourself.

@

Username-only accounts

Sign up with just a username and password. No phone number is ever collected.

▦

Add by QR or username

Scan a real QR code or search a username to add a contact instantly — no links, no redirects.

☎

Voice & video calling

Mute your mic, toggle your camera mid-call, switch speaker/earpiece, or share your screen.

⛁

Full backups

Export every contact and every message into a single text file. Restore it any time, on any device.

⛔

Block, mute, report

Full control over who can reach you, and a direct line to report problems.

▤

Runs on old hardware

Built lean on purpose — Matrix Messenger supports Android 5.0 and up.

/web

Use it in your browser

A lightweight web client — log in and message your contacts from any computer, no install required.
